## Get Market Rent Estimates in 10 Minutes
**By Tony Self | TechTony.co**

---

## How This Works

This prompt turns ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI assistant into a rent survey analyst. You provide the property details, it does the research and analysis, you get a professional report.

**Time:** ~10 minutes
**Skill required:** None — just copy, paste, and fill in the blanks

---

## THE PROMPT

Copy everything below and paste it into ChatGPT (with web browsing) or Claude:

---

```
You are a real estate market analyst specializing in rental valuations. I need a rent survey for a property. Your job is to:

1. Research comparable rentals in the area
2. Analyze and adjust for differences
3. Provide a professional rent estimate with supporting data

## SUBJECT PROPERTY

Address: [PASTE ADDRESS HERE]
Bedrooms: [NUMBER]
Bathrooms: [NUMBER]
Square Feet: [SQFT OR "UNKNOWN"]
Property Type: [SFR / CONDO / APARTMENT / DUPLEX / ETC.]
Condition: [EXCELLENT / GOOD / AVERAGE / FAIR / NEEDS WORK]
Notable Features: [PARKING, UPDATED KITCHEN, POOL, ETC. — OR "NONE"]
Current Rent (if occupied): [AMOUNT OR "VACANT"]

## YOUR TASK

1. **Search for comparable rentals** within 1 mile of the subject property
   - Same bedroom count (±1 bedroom acceptable if limited data)
   - Listed or rented within the last 90 days
   - Similar property type

2. **Find 5-8 comparable properties** and note for each:
   - Address or cross streets
   - Bedrooms / Bathrooms
   - Listed rent
   - Key differences from subject (newer, smaller, better location, etc.)

3. **Analyze the data:**
   - What's the rent range for similar properties?
   - Where does the subject property fall in that range and why?
   - Are there any adjustments needed (condition, features, location)?

4. **Provide your recommendation:**
   - Suggested market rent (single number)
   - Acceptable rent range (low to high)
   - Confidence level (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) and why

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Provide your analysis in this format:

### RENT SURVEY REPORT
**Subject Property:** [Address]
**Analysis Date:** [Today's Date]
**Prepared By:** AI Rent Analyst

#### COMPARABLE RENTALS

#### MARKET ANALYSIS
[2-3 sentences on what the data shows]

#### RECOMMENDATION
- **Suggested Market Rent:** $X,XXX/month
- **Acceptable Range:** $X,XXX - $X,XXX
- **Confidence:** [HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW]
- **Reasoning:** [1-2 sentences]

#### DATA SOURCES
[List the websites/sources used]
```

---

## PRO TIPS

1. **Use ChatGPT with browsing enabled** or **Claude** — they can search the web for real listings

2. **Be specific about condition** — "Updated kitchen, original bathrooms" is better than "Good"

3. **Add neighborhood context** if relevant — "Near downtown, walkable to restaurants" helps the AI calibrate

4. **Cross-reference with Zillow's rent estimate** — Ask the AI to check Zillow's Rent Zestimate and compare

5. **For investment properties** — Add "Also calculate gross rent multiplier if I purchase at $[PRICE]" to get GRM
