## What You'll Get

A Gemini Gem that:
- Searches for current rental listings automatically
- Finds 5 comparable rentals within 1 mile
- Creates a comparison table
- Gives a specific rent recommendation

## Requirements
- Google account (FREE tier works!)
- 10 minutes to set up

## Why Gemini?
- **Free web search** — No subscription required
- **Google ecosystem** — Integrates with Drive, Gmail, etc.
- **Fast setup** — Simpler than ChatGPT GPTs
